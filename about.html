Our Beginning A Stitch of Passion

in Tanjung Batu

La Fyshionzè Tailor was founded with a dream — to bring world-class ladies tailoring to the heart of Tanjung Batu, Kepulauan Riau. What began as a small atelier in Sawang has grown into the most trusted fashion destination in Kundur and Karimun.

Our founder's passion for fashion design and precision craftsmanship drove her to create a space where women of Tanjung Batu could experience truly personalized tailoring — garments made not just to fit the body, but to fit the soul.

Today, La Fyshionzè Tailor — fyshionze.store — serves brides, professionals, and fashion-forward women across Sawang, Tanjung Batu, Kundur, Karimun, and beyond Kepulauan Riau.

Our Craft The Art of Fine

Tailoring

At La Fyshionzè Tailor, every stitch is intentional. We believe that great tailoring is an art form — a dialogue between the tailor's skill and the client's vision.

Our process begins with listening. We take time to understand your style, your occasion, and your body — then we translate that understanding into a garment that exceeds every expectation.

We source quality fabrics — batik, organza, lace, silk blends, and modern textiles — ensuring that every custom dress in Tanjung Batu carries the La Fyshionzè mark of excellence.
